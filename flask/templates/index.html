Life Expectancy Predictor

Becky Peters, Galvanize Capstone 2 Regression Project


The purpose of this project is to better understand the methods and outcomes of regression analysis using machine learning techniques and to do inferential modeling to discover the most important features when predicting life expectancy for different US populations. The data on which the model is based comes from the National Longitudinal Mortality Survey, a project from the US Census consisting of a "database developed for the purpose of studying the effects of demographic and socio-economic characteristics on differentials in U.S. mortality rates." (Quote taken from their website.)
